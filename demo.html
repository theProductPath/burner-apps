Healthcare Data Review
Let's walk through one of these problems end-to-end — from raw data to structured results.
The Setup

You're the Coordinator — a clinical data manager with 918 records that need human review before a system migration. Patients, doctors, appointments, prescriptions — all interconnected, all needing expert eyes.

Your reviewers — the Consumers — are domain experts, not data people. You need them to find problems and report back in a way you can actually use.

Coordinator sends it out, gets results back
Consumer reviews the data, flags issues
Coordinator's Problem

This is what you send out today

As the Coordinator, you email a 918-record spreadsheet and hope for the best. Five sheets, dozens of columns, relationships buried across tabs — and you're asking Consumers who aren't data people to make sense of it.

The Burner App Principles

Disposable by Design
Build tools that are perfect for right now. When you stop building things that last forever, you build things that actually get used.
Zero-Friction Delivery
Open and start working. No login, no installation, no training. If it requires explanation, it's too complicated.
Artifacts Over Applications
What matters is the structured output — a decision made, data validated, issues surfaced. The deliverable outlives the tool.
AI Makes It Possible
Purpose-built disposable tools used to be economically absurd. AI-assisted development makes them practical, fast, and accessible to non-developers.
